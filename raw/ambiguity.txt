## Introduction {#sec:intro}

* [Language yab](LanguageTable?#cldf:yab) ([yaba1248](https://glottolog.org/resource/languoid/id/yaba1248)) is an underdescribed Cariban language of Venezuela.
* [matter2022uniparser](sources.bib?with_internal_ref_link&ref#cldf:matter2022uniparser) is a morphological parser for [Language yab](LanguageTable?#cldf:yab), implemented with [uniparser-morph](https://github.com/timarkh/uniparser-morph)
* for many forms, the parser suggests multiple possible analyses
* this paper discusses these ambiguous forms, the reasons behind them, and the implemented solutions

## The ambiguities

### Accidental
* *makë*: 'mother' or ma-kë 'throw!'
* *yaka*: 'excavate' or 'ALL'
* *ma*: 'throw-NMLZ' or 'RESTR'? 
    * safe to remove it after [Morpheme tawara-too](MorphsetTable?#cldf:tawara-too):
    `REMOVE ("ma" vt) IF (-1 ("tawara"));` 
* syllable reduction leads to homophonous [Morph wej-cop-0](MorphTable?#cldf:wej-cop-0) and _wej_ 'come'
* [Morpheme teloc](MorphsetTable?#cldf:teloc) and [Morpheme tenpert](MorphsetTable?#cldf:tenpert)
* [Morpheme yakaall](MorphsetTable?#cldf:yakaall) and _yaka_ 'deer' and _yaka_ 'excavate'
* [Morph t3](MorphTable?#cldf:t3)_aro_ '3-rice' or just [Morpheme taro-say](MorphsetTable?#cldf:taro-say) 'say'?
* *yakara=ma* 'believe=<span class="smallcaps">rst</span>' or [Morpheme yakarama-tell](MorphsetTable?#cldf:yakarama-tell) 'tell'?
